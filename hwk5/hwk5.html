Yesterday's Top 5 Stories

JavaScript Voted Best Language

Javascript was just voted the best computer language to learn by The Daily Clarion. Researchers found that employees that knew JavaScript received a 2% pay increase when compared to peers that did not know JavaScript.

Bitcoin's Price Hovering at Around $10,000

For the last week Bitcoin's price has been fairly stagnant, hovering around the 10k mark. The high for last week was $11,927.00 and the low was $9,425.00.

Longhorns Must Win

The Longhorns must win one of their last two games if they hope to make the NCAA March Madness Playoffs. Their next game is against highly ranked Kansas, in which they are the underdogs.

Trump Bans Schools

In order to eliminate mass school shootings, President Trump has decided to remove schools from the equation. "If there are no schools there can't be school shootings.", the President said in a statement to the country.

Team USA finishes Olympics with 23 Medals

Team USA finished fourth overall with 23 medals in the Pyeongchang Winter Olympics. The medal count was as follows: 9 Gold Medals, 8 Silver Medals, and 6 Bronze Medals.

Today's Top 3 Stories

President Trump Visits Florida

Today, President Trump visited the Florida town of Parkland, which is the town where the shooting at Marjory Stoneman Douglas High School occured. He met with several of the families of students that attend the school.

Marvel's Black Panther a Massive Success

Black Panther has found major success in its second week, topping $700 Million in ticket sales. The movie has been praised as diverse, deep, and full of great action.

Olympic Games a Huge International Success

One of the longest, most successful Olympics in modern history came to a close today at the closing ceremonies in Pyeongchang. The Games were a great example of world harmony and peace as North Korea and South Korea walked out as one at the openning of the games.
